# Combining Chippewa into DND

## Abilities
* Strength
* Dexterity
* Constitution
* Intelligence
* Wisdom
* Charisma

## Classes
* Classes will be the pathways and 
  * Pathways - See Chippewa Universe/pathways.md

 ## Spells & Skills
 Spells and Skills from the DnD system will be transferred over directly, especially for the Waabishkiiwe. Spells should have slight modifications, however, in how they present themselves for those using the pathways and using Anishinaabemowin to name the spells. Examples are provided below. Another unique aspect of spellcasting in Chippewa, is that custom spells utilizing Anishinaabemowin are heavily encouraged. If a player comes forward with a spell named using the language (extra bonus points for using the language in the description too), then the Dungeon Master should encourage that (while hopefully not breaking the game). One spell per level is the encouraged rate for this, however.

 ## Species
 Species will be primarily human, though this may be flexible.
